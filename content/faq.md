---
title: "Frequently Asked Questions"
layout: "faq"
faqs:
  - q: "How do I apply for a new license or permit?"
    a: "You can apply by going to the 'Apply' section of the EDGE Portal. Select the service you need, fill out the required information, and upload the necessary documents."
  - q: "How do I track my application?"
    a: "Visit the 'Track Application' page and enter your Application Reference Number (ARN) provided during submission."
  - q: "What should I do if my application is marked as 'Returned'?"
    a: "An application is usually returned due to incomplete or incorrect requirements. Check your email for a detailed notification from the NTC evaluator and follow the instructions to re-submit."
  - q: "Can I pay for my fees online?"
    a: "The portal currently provides an Order of Payment. You may pay through various authorized payment channels listed in the payment instructions sent to your email."
  - q: "How long does the evaluation process take?"
    a: "Processing times vary depending on the service. Generally, regular applications take 3 to 5 working days from receipt of complete requirements."
---

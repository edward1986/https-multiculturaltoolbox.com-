---
title: "Frequently Asked Questions"
layout: "faq"
faqs:
  - q: "How do I apply for a service?"
    a: "To apply, navigate to the 'Services' page to find your required transaction. Click 'Apply Now' to be guided through our multi-step application portal. You can also go directly to the 'Apply' page and select your service from the list."
  - q: "How do I track my application?"
    a: "After submitting your application, you will receive a unique Application Reference Number (ARN). Visit the 'Track Application' page and enter your ARN to see the real-time status of your request, from initial evaluation to final approval."
  - q: "What documents are required?"
    a: "Documentary requirements vary depending on the service. Detailed checklists are available on each service's specific information page. Generally, you will need digital copies (PDF or JPG) of identification, technical specifications, and relevant certifications."
  - q: "Where can I find my reference number?"
    a: "Your Application Reference Number (ARN) is displayed on the success confirmation screen immediately after submission. It is also sent to the email address you provided in the application form. Please keep this number for all future inquiries."
  - q: "What should I do if my application is incomplete or returned?"
    a: "If an evaluator determines that your submission is incomplete or requires clarification, your status will change to 'Returned for Correction'. You will receive an email notification detailing the missing requirements or necessary adjustments. You can then re-submit the corrected documents through the portal."
  - q: "How do I contact support?"
    a: "If you encounter technical issues or have specific questions about your application, you can reach our support team via email at support@ntc.gov.ph or call our consumer hotline at 1682. For regional concerns, please refer to the Regional Office Directory on our Contact page."

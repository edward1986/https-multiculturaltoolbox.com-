---
title: "Frequently Asked Questions"
layout: "faq"
faqs:
  - q: "How do I start an application?"
    a: "To start an application, go to the 'Services' page, find the service you need, and click 'Apply Now'. Alternatively, you can go directly to the 'Apply' page and select your desired service from the dropdown menu."
  - q: "How do I track my application status?"
    a: "Once you submit an application, you will receive an Application Reference Number (ARN). Go to the 'Track Application' page and enter this number to see the current status and history of your request."
  - q: "What documents are required for my application?"
    a: "Requirements vary by service. You can find a detailed list of documentary requirements on the specific service detail page under the 'Services' section."
  - q: "Where can I find my Application Reference Number (ARN)?"
    a: "Your ARN is displayed on the confirmation screen immediately after submission and is also sent to the registered email address you provided in the application form."
  - q: "What should I do if my application is returned?"
    a: "If an application is marked as 'Returned for Correction', check your email for specific notes from the evaluator. You will need to log in (if applicable) or re-upload the correct documents as specified in the notice."
  - q: "How do I pay the processing fees?"
    a: "After your documents are verified, you will receive an 'Order of Payment' via email. This document contains instructions on how to pay through various authorized online and offline payment channels."
  - q: "Who can I contact for technical support?"
    a: "For technical issues with the EDGE Portal, please email support@ntc.gov.ph or call our hotline at (02) 8924-4008 during office hours (Monday to Friday, 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM)."

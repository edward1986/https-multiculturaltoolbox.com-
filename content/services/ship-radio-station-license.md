title: "Ship Radio Station License"
description: "Application for issuance or renewal of Radio Station License for various types of vessels, including fishing, commercial, and passenger ships."
category: "Permits and Licenses"
eligibility: "Owners or operators of Philippine-registered vessels required to carry radio equipment as per international and national regulations."
processing_time: "10-14 Working Days"
fees: "Refer to MC No. 19-12-2002 for Ship Station Fees"
requirements:
  - Duly accomplished application form
  - Valid Ship Station License (for renewal)
  - Copy of Certificate of Philippine Registry
  - Inspection Report from NTC Technical Staff
  - List of Radio Equipment with Technical Specifications
steps:
  - Submit the application for Ship Radio Station License through the portal.
  - Upload the required vessel and equipment documents.
  - Pay the evaluation and inspection fees.
  - Coordinate with the NTC regional office for the physical inspection of the vessel's radio equipment.
  - Issuance of the Ship Radio Station License upon successful inspection and evaluation.

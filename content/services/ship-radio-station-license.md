The **Ship Radio Station License** is a mandatory requirement for all Philippine-registered vessels equipped with radio communication apparatus. This ensures that maritime radio operations comply with international standards set by the International Telecommunication Union (ITU) and the International Maritime Organization (IMO).

### Global Maritime Distress and Safety System (GMDSS)
Vessels engaged in international or domestic voyages must comply with GMDSS requirements. NTC ensures that the radio equipment installed on these vessels is capable of providing distress, urgency, and safety communications at all times.

### Inspection and Certification
Before the issuance or renewal of a license, vessels may be subject to a physical inspection by NTC maritime engineers to verify the operational status of the radio equipment and the proficiency of the radio operators on board.

### Call Sign and MMSI Assignment
The NTC is the sole authority in the Philippines responsible for assigning Maritime Mobile Service Identities (MMSI) and Call Signs to vessels. These identifiers are crucial for global tracking and identification during maritime emergencies.

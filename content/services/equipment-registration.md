The **Equipment Registration** process (Type Approval and Type Acceptance) is essential for maintaining the quality and compatibility of telecommunications equipment in the Philippine market. It ensures that all devices sold and used in the country meet technical standards for electromagnetic compatibility and safety.

### Type Approval vs. Type Acceptance
- **Type Approval:** Issued for equipment that has been tested in NTC-recognized laboratories to ensure compliance with Philippine standards.
- **Type Acceptance:** Issued for equipment that has already been approved by recognized international regulatory bodies or laboratories, where the NTC accepts their test reports as proof of compliance.

### Mandatory Compliance
No telecommunications or radio equipment shall be sold, distributed, or used in the Philippines without the proper NTC Type Approval or Type Acceptance certificate. This includes mobile phones, Wi-Fi routers, bluetooth devices, and other wireless peripherals.

### Dealer and Manufacturer Responsibility
Licensed dealers and manufacturers are responsible for ensuring that the equipment they offer to the public is properly registered with the NTC. Each approved device must bear the official NTC Type Approval or Type Acceptance label.

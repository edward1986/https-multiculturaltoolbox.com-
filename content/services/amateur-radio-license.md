The **Amateur Radio License** is issued to qualified individuals who have demonstrated their knowledge of radio rules, regulations, and technical theory. This license allows the holder to operate a radio station for self-training, intercommunication, and technical investigation.

### Classes of Amateur License
1. **Class A (Advanced):** Highest class with full privileges.
2. **Class B (General):** Intermediate class.
3. **Class C (Technician):** Entry-level class for VHF/UHF operation.
4. **Class D (Foundation):** Basic entry-level.

### Importance of Licensing
Licensing ensures that amateur operators are aware of the proper use of frequencies and do not cause interference to other services. Amateurs play a vital role in providing emergency communications during disasters.

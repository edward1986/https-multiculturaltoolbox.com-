The NTC regulates the deployment of **Satellite Earth Stations** to ensure efficient use of orbital resources and to prevent interference with terrestrial radio networks. This service is critical for providing connectivity to remote and underserved areas of the Philippines.

### Technical Coordination
Each earth station application is evaluated for compatibility with existing satellite and terrestrial services. Applicants must provide detailed link budget analysis and antenna radiation patterns to facilitate the technical review.

### Service Scope
This license covers both Receive-Only and Transmit-Receive earth stations operating in various frequency bands (C-band, Ku-band, Ka-band).

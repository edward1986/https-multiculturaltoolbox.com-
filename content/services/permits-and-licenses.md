---
title: "Permits and Licenses"
description: "Acquire permits for the purchase, possession, and operation of radio communication equipment."
category: "Permits and Licenses"
eligibility: "Entities or individuals intending to operate radio communication networks or equipment."
requirements:
  - Network Diagram
  - Technical Specifications of Equipment
  - SEC Registration or DTI Permit (for businesses)
  - Proof of passing the required NTC hearings (if applicable)
steps:
  - Select the type of Permit or License required.
  - Complete the online technical data sheets.
  - Upload supporting legal and technical documents.
  - Submit and wait for evaluation.
  - Pay the corresponding fees upon receiving the Order of Payment.
---

processing_time: "10-15 Working Days"
fees: "Based on NTC Fee Schedule (MC No. 19-12-2002)"
requirements:
  - Letter of Intent addressed to the Commissioner
  - Duly accomplished application form
  - Engineering Plans (for new stations)
  - SEC Registration or DTI Business Name
  - Franchise or Authorization (if applicable)
steps:
  - Select the specific permit or license type in the portal.
  - Provide detailed technical specifications of the radio equipment.
  - Upload necessary corporate and technical documents.
  - Undergo technical evaluation by the NTC engineering department.
  - Pay the prescribed fees upon receipt of the Order of Payment.
  - Wait for the release of the Permit or License.

---
title: "Radio Dealer / Service Center"
description: "Licensing and registration of radio communication equipment dealers, manufacturers, and service centers."
category: "Other public transactions"
eligibility: "Business entities intending to engage in the sale, repair, or manufacture of radio communication equipment."
processing_time: "5-10 Working Days"
fees: "PHP 2,000.00 - PHP 5,000.00 (MC No. 19-12-2002)"
requirements:
  - Duly accomplished application form
  - SEC Registration or DTI Business Name
  - Mayor's Permit
  - List of technical personnel and equipment
  - Location plan and photos of the establishment
steps:
  - Submit the dealer/manufacturer application through the EDGE Portal.
  - Upload necessary corporate and technical documents.
  - Pay the prescribed fees upon receipt of the Order of Payment.
  - On-site inspection of the establishment by NTC personnel (if required).
  - Issuance of the Dealer/Service Center Permit.

---
title: "Deadline for Renewal of Radio Licenses"
date: 2024-05-10
important: false
summary: "Reminder to all radio license holders regarding the upcoming renewal deadline."
---
The National Telecommunications Commission (NTC) reminds all radio license holders that the deadline for the renewal of licenses expiring in June 2024 is **May 31, 2024**.

Failure to renew on or before the deadline may result in surcharges and penalties. We encourage all licensees to use the **EDGE Portal** for a faster and more convenient renewal process.

Please ensure that all required documents are ready before starting your online application.

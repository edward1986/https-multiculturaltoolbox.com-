---
title: "Public Notice: New Frequency Allocation"
date: 2024-05-10
important: false
summary: "The NTC announces the new frequency allocation for terrestrial broadcasting services to improve signal quality."
---
The National Telecommunications Commission (NTC) hereby informs the public and all stakeholders of the new frequency allocation for terrestrial broadcasting services. This move is part of the Commission's ongoing efforts to optimize spectrum usage and improve signal quality for consumers nationwide.

All affected licensees are advised to coordinate with their respective Regional Offices for the technical transition plan.

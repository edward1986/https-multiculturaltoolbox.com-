Welcome to the **NTC EDGE Portal**! This guide is designed to help you navigate our new digital service platform efficiently.

### 1. Browse Services
Before starting an application, visit the **Services** page to see the full list of regulatory transactions available online. Each service page provides detailed information on eligibility, requirements, and processing steps.

### 2. Prepare Documents
All requirements must be submitted in digital format (PDF, JPG, or PNG). Ensure that your scans are clear and readable to avoid delays in evaluation. The maximum file size per document is 5MB.

### 3. Start Your Application
Click the **Apply Now** button on any service page or use the **Apply** menu. Follow the interactive multi-step process:
- **Select Service & Region:** Choose the correct regional office.
- **Fill Form:** Provide accurate personal and technical details.
- **Upload:** Securely attach your documentary requirements.
- **Review:** Double-check all information before final submission.

### 4. Track Your Progress
After submission, you will receive an **Application Reference Number (ARN)**. Use the **Track Application** feature to monitor the real-time status of your request.

For further assistance, you may consult our [FAQ](/faq) or [Contact Support](/contact).

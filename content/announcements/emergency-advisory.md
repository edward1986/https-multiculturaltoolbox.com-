---
title: "Emergency Advisory: Typhoon Preparedness"
date: 2026-05-18T10:00:00+08:00
important: true
summary: "NTC directs all telecommunications entities to ensure network resilience and personnel safety ahead of the upcoming typhoon."
---

In anticipation of the upcoming typhoon, the **National Telecommunications Commission (NTC)** hereby directs all public telecommunications entities (PTEs) to take necessary measures to ensure the continuous operation and resilience of their networks and facilities.

**Directives:**
1. **Ensure Network Stability:** Deploy standby generators and ensure sufficient fuel supply at all critical sites.
2. **Personnel Safety:** Mobilize technical teams while prioritizing their safety and provide them with necessary protective equipment.
3. **Public Communication:** Maintain open communication channels with the public and relevant government agencies for timely updates on network status.
4. **Restoration Readiness:** Prepare quick-response teams and spare parts for immediate restoration of any damaged infrastructure once it is safe to do so.

Regional offices are likewise instructed to coordinate with local government units and disaster risk reduction management councils in their respective jurisdictions.

The NTC remains committed to ensuring that vital communication services remain available to the Filipino people during times of emergency.

## 1. Commitment to Data Privacy

The National Telecommunications Commission (NTC) is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal data is handled in accordance with the *Data Privacy Act of 2012 (RA 10173)*.

## 2. Collection of Information

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide when applying for regulatory services, including:
- Full Name and Contact Details
- Identification Documents
- Technical Specifications of Radio Equipment
- Location Data for Radio Stations

## 3. Purpose of Processing

Your data is processed solely for the following purposes:
- Evaluation of applications for certificates, permits, and licenses.
- Verification of technical compliance with NTC standards.
- Maintenance of official regulatory records.
- Communication regarding the status of your applications.

## 4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

NTC does not share your personal data with third parties unless required by law or necessary for the performance of our regulatory functions. Any sharing is conducted through secure channels and under strict confidentiality agreements.

## 5. Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. This includes encryption, firewalls, and secure access controls.

## 6. Your Rights

As a data subject, you have the right to:
- Be informed about the collection and processing of your data.
- Access and correct your personal information.
- Object to processing or request the erasure of your data under specific conditions.

## 7. Contact Us

For any privacy-related concerns or inquiries, please contact our Data Protection Officer at `dpo@ntc.gov.ph`.

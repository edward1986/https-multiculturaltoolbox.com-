About the NTC EDGE Portal

The Electronic Data Governance and Evaluation (EDGE) System is the official digital service portal of the National Telecommunications Commission (NTC). It is designed to streamline regulatory processes, improve data integrity, and provide a transparent platform for the telecommunications industry and the general public.

Our Commitment

In line with the Republic Act No. 11032, also known as the Ease of Doing Business and Efficient Government Service Delivery Act of 2018, the NTC is committed to transforming its frontline services through digitalization. The EDGE Portal serves as the primary gateway for:

  • Online filing of applications for permits, licenses, and certificates.
  • Real-time tracking of application status.
  • Secure digital document submission.
  • Transparent fee assessment and payment instructions.

The National Telecommunications Commission

The NTC is the government agency responsible for the supervision, adjudication and control over all telecommunications services throughout the Philippines. Our mission is to maintain a responsive regulatory environment for an effective telecommunications and information infrastructure.

Mission

To maintain a responsive regulatory environment for an effective telecommunications and information infrastructure.

Vision

A world-class regulatory agency for a digitally-empowered Philippines.

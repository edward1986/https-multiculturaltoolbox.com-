---
title: "Equipment Registration"
description: "Registration of customer premises equipment (CPE) and other telecommunications hardware."
category: "Equipment Registration"
eligibility: "Suppliers, dealers, and end-users of regulated telecommunications equipment."
requirements:
  - Equipment Invoice / Receipt
  - NTC Type Approval/Acceptance Certificate
  - Photos of the equipment with Serial Number
steps:
  - Register the equipment details online.
  - Upload proof of purchase and type approval.
  - Submit for verification.
  - Receive the digital Certificate of Registration.
---

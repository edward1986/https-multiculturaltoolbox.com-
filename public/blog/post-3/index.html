How to Optimize Your Site for SEO

Search Engine Optimization (SEO) is the process of improving your website’s visibility on search engines like Google.

While SEO can be complex, there are several basic things you can do to improve your ranking:

  • Use descriptive titles and meta descriptions: Make sure your pages have clear and relevant titles.
  • Optimize image alt tags: This helps search engines understand what’s in your images.
  • Improve site speed: Faster sites generally rank higher.
  • Create high-quality content: Search engines prioritize sites that provide value to users.

With TechFlow and Hugo, you’re already off to a great start with a fast and well-structured site!

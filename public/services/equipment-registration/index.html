Equipment Registration

Registration of customer premises equipment (CPE) and other telecommunications hardware.

Who may apply

Suppliers, dealers, and end-users of regulated telecommunications equipment.

Documentary Requirements

  • Equipment Invoice / Receipt
  • NTC Type Approval/Acceptance Certificate
  • Photos of the equipment with Serial Number

Processing Steps

  1. Step 1: Register the equipment details online.
  2. Step 2: Upload proof of purchase and type approval.
  3. Step 3: Submit for verification.
  4. Step 4: Receive the digital Certificate of Registration.

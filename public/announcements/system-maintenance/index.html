Published on May 15, 2024 IMPORTANT ADVISORY

Scheduled System Maintenance

The NTC EDGE Portal will be temporarily unavailable on May 20, 2024 (Monday) from 8:00 PM to 12:00 AM due to scheduled system maintenance and security updates.

During this period, users will not be able to:

  • Submit new applications
  • Track existing applications
  • Upload documents

We apologize for any inconvenience this may cause and thank you for your patience as we work to improve our digital services.

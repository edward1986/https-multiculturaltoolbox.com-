About the NTC EDGE Portal

Modernizing regulatory services through the Electronic Data Governance and Evaluation (EDGE) System.

The NTC EDGE Portal is the official digital gateway of the National Telecommunications Commission (NTC). It represents a significant step in our commitment to digital transformation, aiming to provide a more efficient, transparent, and accessible platform for all regulatory transactions in the telecommunications sector.

Public Service Commitment

In adherence to the Ease of Doing Business and Efficient Government Service Delivery Act of 2018 (RA 11032), the EDGE Portal is designed to:

  • Reduce Processing Time: Streamlining internal workflows to ensure faster turnaround for all applications.
  • Enhance Transparency: Providing real-time status updates and clear requirements for every service.
  • Ensure Data Integrity: Implementing secure data governance standards to protect applicant information and regulatory records.
  • Eliminate Red Tape: Removing unnecessary steps and digitizing documentary requirements.

The National Telecommunications Commission

The NTC is the primary regulatory body in the Philippines responsible for the supervision, adjudication, and control of all telecommunications services. We strive to foster a competitive environment that encourages the growth of the telecommunications and information technology industries while protecting the interests of the public.

🎯 Mission

To maintain a responsive regulatory environment for an effective telecommunications and information infrastructure through the exercise of its quasi-judicial and quasi-legislative powers.

🌟 Vision

By 2025, the NTC shall be a world-class regulatory agency providing an environment for a digitally-empowered Philippines.

Core Values

Integrity • Professionalism • Accountability • Innovation
